# 🔐 Enable Gmail 2-Factor Authentication - Step by Step

## What is 2FA?
2-Factor Authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your Google account. It's required to generate App Passwords for sending emails.

---

## 📋 Step-by-Step Instructions

### **Step 1: Open Google Account Security**

1. **Open your web browser**
2. **Go to**: https://myaccount.google.com/security
3. **Sign in** with your Gmail account if prompted

---

### **Step 2: Navigate to 2-Step Verification**

1. **Scroll down** to the "Signing in to Google" section
2. **Click on "2-Step Verification"**
   - It should be near the top of the page
   - If you see "Off", click it to enable

---

### **Step 3: Get Started**

1. **Click the "Get Started" button** (blue button)
2. **You'll be asked to sign in again** - Enter your Gmail password
3. **Click "Next"**

---

### **Step 4: Add Phone Number**

1. **Enter your phone number**
   - Select your country code (e.g., +1 for USA, +91 for India)
   - Type your mobile number
2. **Choose how you want to get codes**:
   - **Text message** (recommended) - SMS to your phone
   - **Voice call** - Google calls you with the code
3. **Click "Next"**

---

### **Step 5: Verify Your Phone**

1. **Google will send you a verification code**
   - Check your phone for an SMS
   - Or answer the phone call
2. **Enter the 6-digit code** in the box
3. **Click "Next"**

---

### **Step 6: Turn On 2-Step Verification**

1. **Click "Turn On"** (blue button)
2. **You'll see a confirmation screen**
3. **Click "Done"**

---

### **Step 7: You're Done! ✅**

You should now see:
- "2-Step Verification is on"
- Your phone number listed
- Options to add backup methods

---

## 🎯 Next Step: Generate App Password

Now that 2FA is enabled, you can generate an App Password:

1. **Go to**: https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords
2. **Sign in** with your Google account
3. **Click "Select app"** → Choose **"Mail"**
4. **Click "Select device"** → Choose **"Other (Custom name)"**
5. **Type**: "Lead Management System"
6. **Click "Generate"**
7. **Copy the 16-digit password** (e.g., `abcd efgh ijkl mnop`)
8. **Remove spaces**: `abcdefghijklmnop`
9. **Save this password** - you'll need it!

## ⚠️ Important Notes

1. **You MUST enable 2FA first** before generating App Passwords
2. **Keep your phone nearby** - you'll need it for verification
3. **Save the App Password** - you won't be able to see it again!
4. **Use the App Password**, NOT your regular Gmail password

---

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "Can't find 2-Step Verification"
- Make sure you're at https://myaccount.google.com/security
- Scroll down to "Signing in to Google" section
- It should be the second option

### "Not receiving verification code"
- Check your phone signal
- Wait 2-3 minutes
- Try "Voice call" instead of "Text message"
- Make sure your phone number is correct

### "Already have 2FA enabled"
- Great! Skip to "Generate App Password" step above
- Go directly to https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords
